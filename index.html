About Muhammad Usman

Muhammad Usman partners with companies that want to transform their retail, hospitality and service experiences. The focus is on combining data, design and culture to build organizations that are fast, customer‑centric and resilient.

From board advisory to hands‑on projects with leadership teams, the work centers on three pillars: strategy, innovation and people. Every project starts with a clear vision and ends with measurable impact.
